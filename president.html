A Letter from the President

Dear RGV Community and Allies,

Welcome to the Rio Grande Valley Stonewall Democrats. As we embark on a new year of civic engagement and advocacy, the importance of our collective voice has never been more evident. Here in South Texas, the LGBTQ+ community faces unique challenges, but we also possess incredible resilience, creativity, and power.

Our Vision for the Future

My vision for this chapter is rooted in action and intersectionality. We cannot fight for LGBTQ+ rights without also standing up for reproductive freedom, immigrant rights, and economic justice. We are building a coalition that reflects the beautiful diversity of the Valley.

  • Electoral Success: Flipping key seats and holding elected officials accountable across Hidalgo, Cameron, Starr, and Willacy counties.
  • Community Defense: mobilized rapid response to anti-LGBTQ+ legislation at the state level.
  • Youth Empowerment: Expanding our college and high school outreach programs to foster the next generation of leaders.

I invite you to join us at our next general meeting to learn more about our strategic plan and how you can get involved. We need block walkers, policy nerds, event planners, and everyday voters who are ready to make a difference.

In Solidarity,

Jane Doe
