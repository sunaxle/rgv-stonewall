Our Platform

We advocate for legislative changes and community standards that guarantee full equity for the LGBTQ+ community in Texas.

Comprehensive Nondiscrimination

We fight to update state and local laws to explicitly protect against discrimination based on sexual orientation and gender identity in employment, housing, and public accommodations. No Texan should be fired or evicted because of who they love or who they are.

Transgender Rights & Healthcare

We fiercely oppose legislative attacks on transgender Texans. We advocate for inclusive healthcare access, affirming environments, and the protection of parents' rights to seek necessary gender-affirming medical care for their children.

Public Education

Schools should be safe havens for every student. We support anti-bullying initiatives, inclusive curricula that acknowledge LGBTQ+ history, and policies that respect a student's gender identity and pronouns.

Intersectionality at the Border

In the RGV, LGBTQ+ issues are inherently tied to immigration and systemic inequity. We advocate for humane border policies and the protection of LGBTQ+ asylum seekers who often face severe violence or discrimination.

Track Legislation

During the legislative session, we monitor bills affecting the LGBTQ+ community and coordinate rapid response testimony in Austin.
