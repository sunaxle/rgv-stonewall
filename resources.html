Community Resources

We are committed to the wellbeing of the LGBTQ+ community in the Rio Grande Valley. Below is a directory of local shelters, medical clinics, and support groups available to you.

Shelters & Housing

🏠

Valley Safe Haven

Location: McAllen, TX

An LGBTQ-affirming emergency shelter providing temporary housing and case management for youth and young adults experiencing homelessness.

Contact Shelter
🏳️‍⚧️

Transitional Living House

Location: Brownsville, TX

Long-term transitional housing specifically designed for transgender individuals, offering job placement support and life skills training.

Learn More

Clinics & Medical Care

⚕️

RGV Equality Clinic

Location: Harlingen, TX

A culturally competent health center offering primary and preventive care, HIV/STI testing, and gender-affirming hormone therapy on an informed-consent basis.

Book Appointment
🧠

Pride Counseling Services

Location: Telehealth & Edinburg, TX

Mental health services provided by LGBTQ+ affirming, trauma-informed therapists. Sliding scale fees available for uninsured community members.

Visit Website

Support Groups & Social Spaces

☕

Queer Coffee Hour

Meets: Every Sunday morning

A casual weekly meetup at affirming local RGV coffee shops. A perfect space to meet new friends, network, and relax in a safe environment.

View Schedule
🫂

Youth Alliance Network

Meets: Bi-weekly on Thursdays

A facilitated support group for true LGBTQ+ teenagers (ages 13-18) to discuss identity, school challenges, and build healthy peer support systems.
