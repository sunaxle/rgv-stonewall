Brand Guidelines

For press and partner organizations, please adhere to our brand guidelines when reproducing the RGV Stonewall logo and materials.

  • Primary Colors: Pride Pink (#ec4899), Deep Violet (#8b5cf6), Stonewall Blue (#3b82f6)
  • Typography: Inter (Headings & Body)
  • Logo Usage: Ensure high contrast on solid backgrounds. Do not compress or stretch logo dimensions.
