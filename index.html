Welcome to the Rio Grande Valley Stonewall Democrats. We are the grassroots political voice for the LGBTQ+ community and allies in South Texas, working tirelessly to elect pro-equality candidates and champion inclusive policies.

Our Mission

We believe that when LGBTQ+ Americans win at the ballot box, all Americans win. The RGV Stonewall Democrats empower our community to participate in the democratic process, holds elected officials accountable, and fights for comprehensive non-discrimination protections across South Texas.
