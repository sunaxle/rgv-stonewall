Where Donations Go

We are entirely volunteer-run. That means 0% of your contributions go to salaries, overhead, or consulting bloat. We aggressively push funds into direct voter contact.

  • 60% - Candidates & Campaigns: Direct contributions to endorsed candidates.
  • 25% - Voter Education: Slate mailers, text banks, and digital ads for get-out-the-vote drives.
  • 10% - Events & Organizing: Venue rentals for forums, Pride tabling materials, and volunteer hydration.
  • 5% - Treasury & Compliance: State pac reporting fees, banking fees, and web hosting.
