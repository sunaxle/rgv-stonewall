Our History

The Stonewall Democrats have their roots in the legacy of the 1969 Stonewall uprising, a pivotal moment in the fight for LGBTQ+ rights. In the Rio Grande Valley, our chapter was founded to provide a strong, organized voice for equality within the local Democratic party.

For over a decade, we have worked to elect pro-equality candidates across Hidalgo, Cameron, Starr, and Willacy counties. We amplify the interconnected stories of trans individuals, queer families, and the Hispanic community to build genuine grassroots momentum.
