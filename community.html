Community Initiatives

Our fight for equality is intersectional. We actively partner with organizations advocating for reproductive justice, immigrant rights, and racial equity across the Valley.

Why Intersectionality Matters

The RGV Stonewall Democrats recognizes that the LGBTQ+ community encompasses people of all races, ethnicities, genders, and socioeconomic backgrounds. We cannot achieve liberation for ourselves without securing the human rights of everyone in our border community.

Our Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) committee ensures that our endorsements, event planning, and outreach strategies explicitly center marginalized voices within the queer community, particularly trans women of color and undocumented LGBTQ+ individuals.

Current Focus Areas

  • Reproductive Freedom: Supporting abortion access funds and clinics in Tamaulipas/Texas borderlands.
  • Immigrant Rights: Advocating for LGBTQ+ asylum seekers currently waiting in Matamoros and Reynosa.
  • Voting Rights: Combating voter suppression tactics targeting college campuses and marginalized colonias.
