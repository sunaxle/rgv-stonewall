David M. — 150+ Doors Knocked

"When I came out, I felt like I couldn't be both openly gay and safely living in the Valley. Registering voters and seeing openly queer candidates on our local ballots changed everything for me. Now, I block walk so the next generation doesn't have to feel that fear."

David is an STC student and has been a core canvasser since 2024.
